Origins of the Thornweaver

Born under the eclipsing moons of the Silver Spires, Valerius was never destined for the mundane priesthood of his kin. His soul vibrated with a dissonant frequency, a call from the Obsidian Grimoire that lay buried beneath the roots of the world.

When he finally touched the cold, crystalline surface of the tome, the forest around him withered into ash, replaced by shadows that obeyed his every command. Now, he wanders the realms as a bridge between the living light and the eternal dark, seeking the lost chapters of his own destiny.
